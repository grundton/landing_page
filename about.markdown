Tim-Tarek Grund is a technologist and musician from Berlin, Germany. His work explores the intersections between performer, technology, interface and sound, as well as the role of performers within the socio-technical network of live-electronics. His work focuses on the application of computer vision, object detection and fiducial markers in musical and creative processes. He has presented his installations and performed at institutions in Berlin, Vienna and Nairobi.
